I was born in Menlo Park, CA and I currently am a senior at Tufts University. I study Computer Science and Human Factors Engineering. Lastly, my passion for automotive engineering has driven my extracurricular projects. I am Captain of Tufts Racing, a 40-person engineering team at Tufts University. The team builds an electric formula car from the ground up and competes in the national Formula Hybrid competition each May.
